What is Donkey Kong Bananza?

Donkey Kong Bananza is Nintendo's highly anticipated 3D platformer launching exclusively for the Nintendo Switch 2 on July 17, 2025. This groundbreaking adventure marks the first original Donkey Kong game since Tropical Freeze (2014) and the first 3D platformer since Donkey Kong 64 (1999).

In this epic underground adventure, players control Donkey Kong as he ventures beneath the surface with young Pauline to recover stolen banana-shaped diamonds called Banandium Gems from villainous apes. The game features revolutionary sandbox-like levels with fully destructible environments, allowing Donkey Kong to smash through most terrain to create his own paths and discover hidden secrets.

Visual Excellence and Design

Donkey Kong Bananza showcases Nintendo's commitment to visual excellence with vibrant, cartoonish graphics and stunning particle effects. The game features flying mud, cracking crystals, and sparkly bananas that create an immersive and tactile experience.

Donkey Kong's character design reflects the updated look from the Super Mario Bros. Movie, featuring expressive eyes and detailed feather-like fur texture that brings the character to life like never before. The first stage, set in a gold mine with Donkey Kong wearing a charming miner's outfit, demonstrates the game's attention to environmental storytelling.

Comparisons and Inspirations

Many gaming enthusiasts have compared Donkey Kong Bananza to Super Mario Odyssey, with some describing it as a spiritual successor. However, this game carves its own path by focusing on Donkey Kong's brute strength and tactile environmental interaction rather than Mario's precision platforming.

The terraforming abilities draw parallels to The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom's Ultrahand mechanic, but instead of assembling objects, Donkey Kong directly edits and manipulates the landscape itself, opening up creative possibilities for environmental puzzle-solving.
